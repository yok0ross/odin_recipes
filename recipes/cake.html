Carrot Cake recipe

Description

Carrot cake recipe - well actuall its Bolognese. All on a webpage that includes a description with two whole paragraphs

Yes... two whole paragraphs

Ingredients

  • 500g Beef mince
  • 500g Pork mince
  • 2 medium sized onions
  • 3 medium sized carrots
  • 3 sticks of celery
  • 500g Passata
  • 3Tbps Tomato paste
  • 150ml Red wine
  • 250ml Beef stock
  • Splash of olive oil"

Steps

  1. Splash of olive oil in large pan over medium high heat.
  2. Add finely chopped onion, carrot and celery. Sweat for 20 minutes
  3. Combine pork and beef in a mixing bowl. Add to pan. Stir freqently to avoid clumping.
  4. Add red wine to pan once juices have all evaporated - about 20 minutes
  5. After wine has also evaporated, add 250ml beef stock, passata and tomata paste.
  6. Stir well. Lower heat and allow to simmer for 2-3 hours stirrign occassionally.
  7. Serve with fresh pasta and dust with finely grated Parmigiano Reggiano
